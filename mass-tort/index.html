Mass Tort

Mass tort litigation consolidates similar individual lawsuits stemming from the same negligence into a single proceeding, streamlining the legal process.

If you've sustained injuries or illnesses due to negligence, Anthony Law Firm can help. Our attorneys specialize in confronting major manufacturers of harmful products, drugs, or devices.

    Types of Mass Torts include, but are not limited to, Product Liability, Pharmaceutical Drugs, Medical Devices, Consumer Products, Toxic Torts, Environmental Pollution, and Industrial Accidents.
