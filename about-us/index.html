One of New Jersey’s most prominent personal injury, mass tort, class action, and environmental attorneys heads the firm’s Roseland, New Jersey office. Mr. Anthony has spent his entire career handling personal injury, environmental contamination, toxic and mass torts, chemical exposure, benzene exposure, asbestos exposure, pharmaceutical torts and products liability litigation.

Mr. Anthony has over 31 years of experience as a trial lawyer, 12 years as a council member and mayor, and has spent his career helping people. He has listened to the needs of others and done his best to represent them and bring them justice.

Protecting people and the environment has been a common theme in his life. Whether your case involves you or a loved one, wronged in some way, Anthony Law Firm is here to help. Al and his team will listen to your needs and provide you with a place where you can be heard and receive the best of care.

Outside the Office

Mr. Anthony is very active in community and civic affairs and was elected to the Livingston Township Council in 2012, 2016 and 2020. He served as mayor in 2016, 2019 and 2024 and deputy mayor in 2015, 2018 and 2023. He and his wife Lori have four beautiful children.
