Committed to helping and investing in people

Meet Robin, a deeply passionate litigation paralegal with more than 13 years of experience. She joined Hendler Flores Law in the spring of 2022 and has participated in civil cases and jury trials in numerous states across the nation.

Robin graduated from St. Mary’s University School of Law in 2015, primarily focusing on personal injury and mass torts. She has extensive knowledge and experience working on personal injury cases, including medical malpractice, product liability, catastrophic injuries, and wrongful death. Her experience handling diverse cases, complex legal issues, and a wide range of clients have all contributed to her professional growth and expertise.

Robin grew up in the idyllic little town of Wimberley, Texas and currently still resides in the hill country with her husband, Cade. They enjoy spending time with their families, traveling, biking, and hiking with their dogs: Frannie Lou and Zeus
