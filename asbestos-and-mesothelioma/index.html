Standing Up For You If You’ve Been Harmed by

Dangerous Products

It happens all too often. With potentially millions of dollars at stake for successfully launching a new product, manufacturers don’t take the time to carefully research the potential dangers and side effects of their products. They’re more concerned with getting the jump on the competition than making sure their products are safe. Or, in order to maximize sales, they take their time warning the public after harmful side effects are discovered. As is often the case with dangerous drugs, they value profits more than people.

If you’ve been harmed by a defective product, you may have the right to compensation for your injuries. Your story matters. Hendler Flores Law has decades of experience holding companies accountable for negligently marketing products and medications that are known to be harmful. Holding these companies accountable can bring you not only the financial recovery you need to make sure you can access the best medical care, but also can bring you peace of mind. Whatever your needs and interests in bringing a legal claim against the manufacturers, we will listen to you and support you.

How Do I Know if I’ve Been Injured by a Defective Product?

If you or a loved one have been injured by the failure or breakage of any particular product, it very well may be caused by defective design, defective manufacturing, or a failure to warn you of any potential dangers. Contact a dangerous product attorney at Hendler Flores Law and we’ll examine the potentially defective product, ask just a few questions, and come to a series of conclusions, which may indeed call for a lawsuit. In any event, the problem becomes our problem and we take these kinds of problems very seriously. We want to hear your story, and there is no risk to you.

How Can Hendler Flores Law Help Me if I’ve Been Injured By a Defective Product?

The policy at Hendler Flores Law is to listen, above all else. We want to hear your story and even facts that may seem inconsequential to you may end up being critical facts. We also want to know exactly what your expectations are so that we can fashion legal strategies based on your unique circumstances. Always remember that we’re on your side, and we’ll always be there for you.

If you or a loved one has been injured by using a defective product, like infant formula or a CPAP machine, we’ll see to it that you are compensated to the fullest extent allowed by law. We will gather evidence, establish the facts, fashion legal strategies, and present you with options based on your unique set of facts. We will advise you which option has the best chance for success and implement the best strategy most likely to get you a favorable settlement or a successful jury verdict.

Featured Client Story

Widow Left to Provide for 3 Daughters After Losing Husband to Mesothelioma

Hendler Flores Law represented the family of a decorated Navy Veteran who was exposed to asbestos while serving on a warship in Vietnam. He later died from mesothelioma, leaving his wife to provide for their 3 young daughters. Scott Hendler and co-counsel Michael Brickman negotiated several settlements with asbestos companies for the family, and also won a $13.8 million verdict against Owens Corning Fiberglas, a company that made asbestos pipe covering for Navy vessels. The compensation recovered was more than enough to secure the financial future of the family.

$13.8 Million Jury Verdict in Mesothelioma Death

Thousands of Central American Banana Workers Develop Sterility from Toxic Pesticide

For more than 3 decades, Scott Hendler has been seeking justice for thousands of Central American banana plantation workers who developed sterility and other severe illnesses after being exposed to a dangerous chemical called DBCP. Even though the chemical had been banned in the US, several US-based multinational corporations continued to use it at their international plantations. Despite numerous legal obstacles and devious tactics by defendants, Scott continues to fight for these victims' rights.

Precedent-Setting US Supreme Court Victory
One of the Most Litigated Toxic Tort Cases in History

New Mexico Family Loses 2 Teenage Daughters in Collision With Drunk Driver

Hendler Flores Law and co-counsel represented a Navajo family that was involved in a catastrophic collision with an impaired driver. The driver had been drinking for several hours at 2 different establishments before getting behind the wheel. Two teenage girls were killed in the crash, which also left other family members critically injured. Our litigation team filed several lawsuits on behalf of the family, recovering a total of $30 million in compensation and securing seed money for the establishment of a charity foundation in honor of the family.

$30 Million Recovery in Impaired Driving Case
