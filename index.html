Our Success Stories

Single Mother Struggles to Support Family After Losing Husband to Chemical Exposure

Anthony Law Firm advocated for the family of a skilled factory worker who was exposed to harmful chemicals during his career. After succumbing to a fatal illness caused by this exposure, he left behind his wife and three young children. Al Anthony and co-counsel Michael Brickman secured multiple settlements from the responsible companies, including a $13.8 million verdict against a major chemical manufacturer. The substantial compensation ensured the family's financial stability and future security.

African Farm Workers Suffer Illness from Illegal Pesticide Use

For over 30 years, Al Anthony has fought for justice for African farm workers who suffered serious health issues, including sterility, due to exposure to a banned pesticide. Despite its prohibition in the US, several multinational corporations continued to use the toxic chemical in their African operations. Despite significant legal challenges and opposition, Anthony remains committed to advocating for the rights of these affected workers.

Texas Family Devastated by Fatal Truck Accident Involving Distracted Driver

Anthony Law Firm and co-counsel represented a Texan family who experienced a tragic truck accident caused by a distracted driver. The driver had been using his phone before the collision, resulting in the deaths of two teenage siblings and severe injuries to other family members. Our legal team pursued multiple lawsuits on behalf of the family, securing $30 million in compensation and establishing a charitable foundation in the family's honor.
